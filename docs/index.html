Coronavirus Tracker (PH)

An app for tracking the Novel Coronavirus (COVID-19, SARS-CoV-2) outbreak in the Philippines.

Download APK

Features

1. Current data: Shows the most recent data. (Regularly updating)


2. Charts:

- Line Chart for the number of confirmed, recovered and deaths

- Bar Chart for the percentage of confirmed based on Age

- Pie Chart for the percentage of confirmed based on gender


3. Philippine Cases: Shows all the case confirmed by DOH and its information.


4. Test Results: Shows the summary by test results of confirmed cases, cases tested negative and cases pending test results.


5. Global Cases: Show all the total cases around the world


6. Other Information:

- Cases outside the Philippines

- Facilities

- Metro Manila Checkpoints

- Local Government Units lockdowns
